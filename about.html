About Justin

Justin is An Australian composer & sound designer who specialises in creating music & sound effects for video games, film, animation and other media.

Starting from a young age, Justin has explored the world of sound through multiple avenues including music composition, sound design, field recording, foley and more. 

In 2014, Justin pursued a degree in audio engineering at JMC Academy in Sydney, Australia. During this time, he worked alongside the game design students to help with the music and sound design for their projects. Since then, he has worked on various audio projects including games, films and animations. Justin's main music genres are orchestral, oriental and chillout. He is also skilled in electronic, ambient and folk styles.

Journey for sound

Since 2014, I've travelled across various countries and recorded a great number of sounds. From the soothing forest soundscapes of New Zealand, to the busy streets of Vietnam, and the rowdy taverns in Australia's Snowy Mountains. These recordings have helped me bring fantasy worlds to life. ​​​​​​​

Instrument crafting

Over the years I've tried my hand at crafting a few instruments, such as bamboo flutes, clay xun, and anglo saxon lyres. Playing an instrument that you yourself crafted is a really special feeling.

Sound is the invisible bridge between what we see and what we feel.
